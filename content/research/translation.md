We develop neural machine translation systems for Bengali paired with low-resource languages — a critical gap given that the majority of MT research focuses on English-centric pairs. Our work covers Bengali↔Hindi, Bengali↔Tibetan, Bengali↔Sylheti, and other South Asian and regional language pairs.
