Prof. Das is a leading authority on Bengali dialectology and experimental phonetics. She leads Bāk's fieldwork programs, overseeing corpus collection in Sylheti, Chittagonian, and Rajshahi dialects. Her 2025 Journal of Phonetics paper on Chittagonian tonal variation has been cited 80+ times since publication.
