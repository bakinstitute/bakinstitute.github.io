Our Mission

“Bengali is one of humanity’s great literary languages‚Äîyet it remains underrepresented in computational research. We exist to close that gap, one open dataset at a time.”

01

Open Access Research

All our publications, datasets, and code are freely available under permissive licenses. No paywalls, no gatekeeping.

02

Community-Driven Science

We partner with linguists, technologists, and speakers across Bangladesh, West Bengal, and the diaspora.

03

Dialect Equity

Standard Bengali dominates NLP research. We invest in Sylheti, Chittagonian, Rajshahi, and other regional varieties.
