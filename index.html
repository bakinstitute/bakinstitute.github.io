Our Mission

“Bengali is one of humanity’s great literary languages—yet it remains underrepresented in computational research. We exist to close that gap, one open dataset at a time.”

01

Open Access Research

All our publications, datasets, and code are freely available under permissive licenses. No paywalls, no gatekeeping.

02

Community-Driven Science

We partner with linguists, technologists, and speakers across Bangladesh, West Bengal, and the diaspora.

03

Dialect Equity

Standard Bengali dominates NLP research. We invest in Sylheti, Chittagonian, Rajshahi, and other regional varieties.

Research Areas

Six fields.
One language.

অ
NLP · AI
Natural Language Processing

Building tokenizers, parsers, POS taggers, and language models trained natively on Bengali—not translated from English.

শ
Phonetics · Speech
Speech & Phonology

Acoustic modeling, ASR systems, and TTS engines that capture the full prosodic range of spoken Bengali.

গ
Computational Linguistics
Morphology & Grammar

Formal computational accounts of Bengali’s rich morphological system—agglutinative verb forms, honorific scales, and nominal declension.

ব
Machine Translation
Cross-Lingual Systems

High-quality neural MT between Bengali and low-resource language pairs, with a focus on South and Southeast Asia.

ড
Dialectology
Dialect Documentation

Fieldwork, corpora, and computational tools for underrepresented Bengali dialects facing endangerment or diglossia.

শি
EdTech · Tools
Language Learning Technology

AI-assisted tools for heritage learners, diaspora communities, and literacy programs in under-resourced schools.
