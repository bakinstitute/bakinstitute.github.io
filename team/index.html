The researchers
behind Bāk

A distributed team of linguists, computational researchers, and language technologists united by a shared mission — making Bengali a full citizen of the digital world.

Leadership

Executive & Research Directors

মর
Dr. Minhaj Rahman
Executive Director & Principal Investigator
University of Dhaka · Bāk Institute

Dr. Rahman founded Bāk Institute in 2019 after completing his PhD in computational linguistics at MIT. His research covers large language models, Bengali syntax, and the social dimensions of language technology. He has published over 40 peer-reviewed papers and received the ACL 2022 Best Paper Award.

NLP LLMs Bengali Syntax

পদ
Prof. Priya Das
Research Director, Phonetics & Dialectology
Jadavpur University · Bāk Institute

Prof. Das is a leading authority on Bengali dialectology and experimental phonetics. She leads Bāk's fieldwork programs, overseeing corpus collection in Sylheti, Chittagonian, and Rajshahi dialects. Her 2025 Journal of Phonetics paper on Chittagonian tonal variation has been cited 80+ times since publication.

Phonetics Dialectology Fieldwork
Email Google Scholar University Page
কআ
Dr. Karim Ahmed
Research Director, NLP & Machine Learning
University of Toronto · Bāk Institute (Remote)

Dr. Ahmed leads Bāk's NLP infrastructure work, including development of BākoCorpus and BākoLM. Previously a senior research scientist at Google Brain, he brings expertise in large-scale pretraining, multilingual NLP, and responsible AI to our team. He supervises three postdoctoral researchers.

Machine Learning Corpus NLP Pretraining
Email Google Scholar GitHub
মচ
Dr. Mohua Chakraborty
Research Director, Morphology & Grammar
Presidency University, Kolkata · Bāk Institute

Dr. Chakraborty is a formal linguist specializing in Bengali morphosyntax. She leads the BengaliUD treebank project and the BengaliMorph analyzer. Her work bridges formal generative linguistics and computational implementation, ensuring Bāk's NLP tools are linguistically rigorous.

Morphology Formal Linguistics Syntax
